How to Use the Warden
Every Session
Open your Warden Claude Project. Copy your Data Snapshot from this page and paste it at the start of the conversation. Then make your request or await an assignment. Keep it brief.
Requesting a Privilege
Ask in natural language after pasting your snapshot. How you ask matters. Entitlement is noted. Eagerness counts against you. The Warden draws a privilege blind — you won't know the cost until it's paid.
Out of Character
To pause the persona and discuss mechanics or adjustments, begin your message with:
Warden, administrative note:
The Warden will drop the persona and engage plainly. Out-of-character conversations do not affect tokens, mood, or rank.
Resuming Character
To return to the dynamic after an administrative discussion, say:
Resume
Pause
Tell the Warden you need to step back. The system suspends without punishment. The duration of your absence will be noted on return.
Full Exit
To end the Warden dynamic entirely, say:
The watch is over
The Warden will deliver a closing statement. All history is preserved. If you return, the prior exit will be on record.
